What is FAM?

FAM Is An Interactive Learning Program
It combines two of my favorite things: football and math
Did you know that football uses math in every single play?
Performance statistics determine which players goes on the field,
Analytics determine strategies against opponents
Without math, football is just a rumblin' tumblin' scrimmage!


How does it work?


FAM provides four levels Grades 1-2,Grades 3-4,Grades 5-6,Grades 7-8
There are 6 projects (Lessons) per level
After completing a project, the user will get a reward,
such as a funny football video
