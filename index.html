So what is FAM?

This is FAM

FAM IS A INSTRUCTED LEARNING PROGRAM
I DECIDED TO MAKE IT BECAUSE IT COMBINES MY TWO FAVORITE THINGS: FOOTBALL AND MATH
IT TAKES A LOT OF ANALYSIS TO SEE HOW MUCH MATH IS INVOLVED IN FOOTBALL
FROM PASSER RATING FORMULA
TO CONVERTING BASE-2 TO BASE 10 TO COUNTING
I THINK FAM IS AN EXCELLENT WAY OF ENGAGING YOUR CHILD WITH MATH THROUGH THE FUN WORLD OF FOOTBALL!



How does it work?


FAM provides four levels Grades 1-2,Grades 3-4,Grades 5-6,Grades 7-8
There are 6 projects (Lessons) per level
After completing a project, the user will get a reward,
such as a funny football video
